About

Originally from the North West of England, Matt is now based in Southeast London.
With a focus on portraits, headshots and theatre photography, Matt also enjoys spending time shooting on the streets of London which is where the majority of his day to day images are taken.
Matt has over a decade of experience working in adult social care and this is where he attributes his drive to give each of his clients as much power of their images as possible.
